### This Person Does Not Exist: AI-Generated Non-Existent People

##### https://thispersondoesnotexist.com

This Person Does Not Exist is an AI application website based on StyleGAN technology that generates completely fictional face photos. Each time you refresh the page, it displays a new face image that does not exist in reality. These photos cover different ages, ethnicities, and expressions, showing a high degree of realism. The project demonstrates the application capabilities of Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) in image generation and raises questions about the concept of "authenticity" in the AI era.

### Draw a Fish: Draw a Fish and Let AI Judge Your Drawing

##### https://drawafish.com

Draw a Fish is an interactive AI website where users can draw a fish on the canvas and receive AI-generated ratings and comments on their work. Regardless of drawing skill level, the AI provides feedback. The website combines image recognition technology, allowing users to experience AI evaluation capabilities while being entertained. This application demonstrates the application possibilities of AI in art evaluation.

![Draw a Fish website screenshot](./images/02-drawafish.webp)

### Human or Not: Determine if You're Chatting with a Human or AI

##### https://www.humanornot.ai

Human or Not is a modern version of the Turing test website. Users engage in a short text chat and then need to determine whether the other party is a real person or AI. With the development of large language models like ChatGPT, AI conversational abilities have reached near-human levels, making it genuinely difficult to distinguish at times. The website allows users to experience the current progress level of AI technology and serves as a social experiment project.

![Human or Not website screenshot](./images/03-humanornot.webp)

### 2048 AI: Watch How AI Plays the 2048 Game

##### https://aj-r.github.io/2048-AI

This website demonstrates how AI plays the 2048 game. The website provides multiple AI algorithms, allowing users to select different strategies and observe how AI gradually merges numbers to pursue high scores. The website also supports letting AI run automatically and records the scores it achieves. Through this project, users can learn about different AI decision-making algorithms and game strategies, suitable for those interested in algorithms.

![2048 AI website screenshot](./images/04-2048-ai.webp)

### Flolife: Test Your Life Trajectory with AI

##### https://flolife.me

Flolife is an AI life simulation website. Users can enter personal information and make choices, and the AI will generate a possible life trajectory, including career development, relationships, major events, and other content. The application is based on statistical data and AI reasoning for simulation. While it cannot predict the actual future, it can demonstrate the different outcomes that different choices might bring.

![Flolife website screenshot](./images/05-flolife.webp)

### Pixel Art Generator: Convert Any Image to Pixel Style

##### https://pixelartgenerator.app

Pixel Art Generator is an AI tool that converts photos into pixel art. After uploading an image, the AI automatically converts it into pixel art works with different pixel densities. The tool supports adjusting parameters such as pixel size and color count to create retro-style images. This application is suitable for various purposes including creating game assets and designing avatars.

![Pixel Art Generator website screenshot](./images/06-pixelart.webp)

### Extraordinary Things: Bizarre Objects Generated by AI

##### https://extraordinarythings.co

Extraordinary Things is an art project showcasing AI creativity. The website collects and displays various strange, interesting, and even surreal objects and scenes generated by AI. Content includes fictional creatures, dreamlike architecture, and other elements, with each piece being a unique AI creation. Through this website, users can see the various forms of expression and creative capabilities of AI in artistic creation.
