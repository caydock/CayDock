In the vast ocean of the internet, there are always websites that stand out with their unique interactive features. These websites not only have creative designs but also provide fascinating interactive experiences. Today, let's explore 10 weird websites with fascinating interactive features.

## 1. [Zoomquilt](https://zzz.zoomquilt.org/)

A website that showcases infinite loop images, where users can navigate through endless scenes using the up and down arrow keys. This website creates a visual infinite loop where each zoom takes you deeper into layers of detail, experiencing an endless visual journey. This unique interaction makes you feel like you're in a never-ending visual maze.

---

## 2. [Drawing Garden](https://drawing.garden/)

A small garden built with emojis, where random plants grow when you hover or click with your mouse, accompanied by musical effects. This website transforms simple emojis into a vibrant interactive garden, with each interaction bringing different surprises. The musical effects perfectly combine with visual changes, creating a relaxed and pleasant atmosphere.

---

## 3. [Bruno Simon](https://bruno-simon.com/)

A 2.5D car mini-game where users can control a small car to drive around recklessly in space using the keyboard. This website is not only a creative portfolio but also a fun interactive game. You can drive the car freely in the virtual world and experience unique driving pleasure.

---

## 4. [FFFFidget](https://ffffidget.com/)

An online fidget spinner where users can rotate it with their mouse, simulating the real fidget spinner experience. This website perfectly recreates the spinning sensation of a real fidget spinner, making it an excellent tool for relieving stress and anxiety. Simple interaction design brings unexpected relaxation effects.

---

## 5. [Porthole](https://porthole.leoku.dev/)

A website that simulates an airplane porthole view, where users can choose different landscapes and experience the beauty of the world from high altitude. This website uses unique perspective design to make users feel like they're sitting by an airplane porthole, admiring magnificent scenery from around the world.

---

## 6. [Cat Bounce](https://cat-bounce.com/)

A website that makes cats bounce on the screen, where users can drag cats and observe their bouncing effects. This simple yet fun website brings joyful interactive experiences through cute cat animations. Each drag produces different bouncing effects, making you want to keep playing.

---

## 7. [R. Leonardi's Interactive Resume](http://www.rleonardi.com/interactive-resume/)

An interactive online resume that showcases personal skills and experiences through gamification. This website transforms traditional resumes into a creative interactive experience, where users can learn about the author's skills and experiences through scrolling and clicking, providing a refreshing feeling.

---

## 8. [Species in Pieces](http://species-in-pieces.com/)

A website that showcases endangered species in puzzle form, where users can learn about the status and conservation information of different species through interaction. This website perfectly combines educational content with interactive experiences through unique puzzle art style, allowing users to learn important environmental knowledge while having fun.

---

## 9. [Persepolis Reimagined](https://persepolis.getty.edu/)

A website that recreates the ancient Persepolis ruins, where users can explore the history and architecture of this ancient city through interaction. This website uses advanced 3D technology to allow users to explore the virtual reconstruction of this ancient city immersively, experiencing the charm of history.

---

## 10. [Paper Planes](https://paperplanes.world/)

A global interactive website where users can fold and throw paper planes, sharing and receiving paper planes with users from around the world. This website creates a cross-border interactive platform, allowing users to feel warmth and connection from people worldwide.
